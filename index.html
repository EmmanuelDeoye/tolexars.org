BRIEF HISTORY

Our Story

On September 6, 2022, Tolexars Therapy Services was born from a deep understanding of the resource gaps faced by individuals with disabilities. We are more than an organization; we are a dedicated team that cultivates meaningful partnerships to expand access to essential support. We are committed to fostering an inclusive environment, empowering our community, and driving positive change.

Our Vision

We exist to reach out to people living with disabilities across all institutions through strategic partnerships and collaborations with foundations, schools, and health facilities, creating a world where everyone has access to the support they need.

      Why Volunteer With Us?

      Volunteering with our network is more than giving your time — it’s about creating real impact in the lives of people living with disabilities and underserved communities.

      • Make Real Impact: Support children, adults, and families who truly need care, advocacy, and encouragement.
      • Learn & Grow: Gain hands-on experience in therapy support, community outreach, and disability inclusion.
      • Join a Supportive Community: Work alongside passionate therapists, healthcare professionals, and fellow volunteers.
      • Build Your Profile: Receive recognition, references, and certificates that strengthen your CV and career journey.
      • Flexible Commitment: Volunteer at your own pace — on-site, remotely, short-term or long-term.

      Whether you’re a student, professional, or simply passionate about helping others — there’s a place for you here.
